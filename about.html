Our story

In 2019, we started as a small group of traders and, over time, uncovered the truth behind market movements.

This revelation sparked BehindTheCover — a company dedicated to decoding the stock market's hidden dynamics.
What began as a personal quest has grown into a far more successful project than we could’ve ever imagined.

Today, our team analyzes and copy-trades the market’s most influential forces, specializing in tracking potential insider traders and powerful players who shape prices and turn people into liquidity.
